🌿 Fellowship Phase

1. Type 2. Recovery 3. Updates 4. Undertakings

A Fellowship Phase concludes the Adventuring Phase. Ordinary phases last a week to a season. Yule is the winter end-of-year — ages everyone by 1, restores all Hope, grants WITS bonus Skill Points, and unlocks Yule-only undertakings (Heal Scars, Raise an Heir, Recount a Story).

Yule comes once every ~3 phases. Tap Yule if it's been about a year since your last Yule, or your group is treating this as the winter festival.
